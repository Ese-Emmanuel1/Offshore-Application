About Offshore Logistics

With operations spanning 12 countries and 28 ports worldwide, we are leaders in maritime logistics and offshore support services

Our Mission

To provide reliable, efficient, and innovative offshore logistics solutions that enable our clients to operate safely and successfully in challenging marine environments.

Our Values

Our Core Services

Comprehensive solutions for the offshore energy and maritime industries

Offshore Supply & Logistics

Full-service supply chain management for offshore platforms, including specialized equipment transport and just-in-time delivery.

Port Operations

Comprehensive port management services including cargo handling, storage, and customs clearance for offshore operations.

Rig Support Services

24/7 support for drilling rigs and production platforms including crew changes, waste management, and technical support.

Marine Engineering

Specialized engineering solutions for offshore installations including structural integrity management and equipment maintenance.
